Sardines with tomatoes in the oven


Ingredients
  • 1.5kg Sardine fish
  • 1kg medium tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 thrones thyme
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  •  thyme
method :
  • Cut the tomatoes into circles and clean them from the fry
  • Put it in the oven dish
  • Put on top of it thyme and chopped garlic
  • And two tablespoons of olive oil
  • Put the plate in the oven for 20 to 25 minutes
  • After the tomatoes are done, remove the plate from the oven
  • Then put the sardines and add two tablespoons of the remaining olive oil
  • Place the plate on the side of the grill for 10 minutes, with the addition of black pepper
